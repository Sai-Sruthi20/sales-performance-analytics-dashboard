This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive view of business performance across cities, regions, and customer segments. The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly monitor sales trends, evaluate profitability, and understand customer behavior through visually intuitive reports. It is structured to support data-driven decision-making for both strategic and operational planning.

The first dashboard focuses on overall performance monitoring. It includes visualizations such as sales and profit by city, order date analysis by segment, sales and profit by segment, sales and profit by region, and discount analysis by segment. In addition, KPI cards are used to display key performance indicators including Quality, Minimum, Maximum, and Target values, allowing users to instantly assess whether business performance is meeting expectations.

The second dashboard provides a deeper analytical view of segment and regional behavior. It highlights sales by region and segment, quality by region and segment, profit by segment, and segment distribution by ship mode. This view helps uncover logistics patterns, segment-wise contribution, and regional quality performance, enabling more focused business improvements.

The project also includes a tree graph dedicated to returns analysis. This visualization examines sub-category by return reason, return state by reason, and sub-category by sum of sales. It helps identify products with high return rates, understand the primary causes of returns, and evaluate the financial impact of returned items, supporting continuous quality and operational improvements.
